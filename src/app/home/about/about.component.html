About Us

Established in 2009, QMTIS was founded with the objective to provide a solid platform for ambitious students to learn and network. With over 300 members and 25 events per year QMTIS is the most subscribed and active finance-related society at Queen Mary University of London. We are both events and project-driven. We host weekly events including guest speakers from the financial services industry, CV and Cover Letter workshops as well as Trading Challenges.

Our various sub-divisions such as Investments, Trading, Macro Research and Technology meet up weekly as well to conduct and write research or work on group projects such as M&A models or our own Trading Simulator.

Alumni have gone to work in multinational investment banks, hedge funds, brokerage firms and start-ups.
